# RSL Quantum

**Quantum software, sensing, and post-quantum cryptography — built in India, for India and the world.**

RSL Quantum Private Limited is a deep-tech company spun out of the INTRINSIC Lab at the Indian Institute of Technology Delhi. We work across the full quantum stack — algorithms, hybrid quantum-classical compilation, simulation of quantum sensors, and post-quantum cryptography — with a research-first, benchmarks-first philosophy.

## What we do

| Pillar | What |
|---|---|
| **Algorithms** | Variational methods, error-mitigated kernels, hybrid quantum-classical pipelines |
| **Post-quantum security** | Lattice-based, code-based, hash-based primitives; performance + side-channel analyses |
| **Quantum machine learning** | Hardware-aware kernels, federated quantum learning, expressivity bounds |
| **Sensing simulation** | Noise budgets, drift compensation, atomic-clock and magnetometer modelling |
| **Materials & chemistry** | Tensor-network + first-principles pipelines for India-priority materials |

We aim for honest benchmarks. If quantum isn't better for your problem, we say so.

## Open problems we want help on

A live list lives at [rslquantum.com/research](https://rslquantum.com/research). Bring a problem from your domain — we bring the stack. Student internships (paid, FITT-issued certificate), joint papers, sabbatical visits, and industry pilots are all on the table.

## What lives in this organization

Public supporting work for the RSL Quantum stack:

- **Quantum-India open dataset** — a verified registry of Indian companies, labs, and academic groups working on quantum, with primary-source citations
- **Educational resources** — including *What is Quantum? An Indian Story* (free book for Indian school students, Q3 2026)
- **Verifier tooling** — scrapers and checks used to maintain the Quantum-India dataset
- **Benchmark harnesses** — reproducible scripts for the public benchmarks we cite

Production website, customer-facing services, and proprietary stack are **not** in this org.
